German Pork Schnitzel & Kartoffelpuffer

This two-serving bavarian beauty will take your taste buds on a journey through the German countryside, right from the comfort of your own home. Simple, yet tantalizing, the pairing of fried pork, potato pancakes, and applesauce is a fan-favorite you will not want to miss!


Schnitzel & Kartoffelpuffer Ingredients:

  • 4 Boneless Pork Chops
  • 3 Farm-fresh Eggs
  • 2-3 Large Potatoes
  • 1 White or Yellow Onion
  • 2/3 Cup Flour
  • 2/3 Cup Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Jar of Applesauce
  • 5 Tbsp Cooking Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lemon Wheel & Parsley (optional)
  • Cheesecloth (optional, paper towels otherwise)

Kartoffelpuffer Steps:

  • Shred all of your potatoes with a medium cheese grater. Gather small chunks of potatoes in cheesecloth and squeeze to strain water out. Afterward, lay out on paper towels and pat dry. This is very important! Any moisture in the potatoes will cause your potato pancakes to fall apart in the pan!
  • Place your flour and beaten eggs into 2 separate bowls. Add salt/pepper (to taste) to flour.
  • Heat pan to medium-high heat. Add oil and bring to a simmer.
  • Hand-form flat, palm-sized potato pancakes and carefully place in the oil to fry.
  • Fry until golden-brown, flip, repeat, and remove to a cooling rack or paper towel.
  • Move on to Schnitzel.

Schnitzel Steps:

  1. First up, grab the meat tenderizer and beat these pork chops within an inch of their lives. Be careful not to tear the meat, but these should be about 1/8-1/4 inch thick when you finish. Cover with plastic wrap to make this a cleaner job.
  2. Add your breadcrumbs to a shallow bowl.
  3. Add your pan and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dip your freshly pummelled pork chops in the flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs, and toss into the frying pan.
  5. Fry the pork for about 3-4 minutes, or an internal temperature of 145Â°.
  6. Set schnitzel aside, preferable on a paper towel, cooling rack, or oven rack to prevent them from getting soggy.
  7. Plate and serve hot alongside your Kartoffelpuffer, a small cup of Applesauce, and of course, Bier!
  8. Enjoy!
